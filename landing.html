INTRODUCTION

Doraemon is a cat robot from the 22nd century who has superpowers. He was sent back to help Nobita Nobi, who is a lazy character that always gets himself in trouble. Doraemon has many gadgets that he uses to help Nobita, such as the “bamboo copter”, anywhere door, fast / slow perfume, Gulliver’s tunnel, small light and big light. Doraemon is Nobita's best friend.
